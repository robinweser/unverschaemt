unverschämt. is an independent developer team. It was founded by five single-minded science students.

Skills.

With everyone having his own speciality we complement one another quite well.

Concept

We build software from scratch.
Meaning a well-thought-out concept is inevitable.
UI-Mockups, Data Models & Flow and detailed System Specs is our way to start.

Development

We use scrum methods in combination with planning & tracking tools to keep an continuous development circle.
With Jenkins, Git, Tests & Code reviews we achieve high automation to guarantee good quality.

Technology

We use modern standards to achieve high performance.
Supporting all common plattforms and devices.
We believe in sharing knowledge therefore we to contribute to open source projects.
